
# Eniac Discount Strategy Analysis
Analysis of discount strategies and their impact on revenue in e-commerce

## Overview

This project explores how discount strategies impact revenue in an e-commerce business.

The analysis focuses on understanding whether discounts truly drive sales and how pricing strategies can be improved using data.

---

## Objectives

* Analyze the relationship between discounts and revenue
* Identify which product categories generate the most revenue
* Evaluate how discounting varies across categories
* Provide actionable business recommendations

---

## Dataset

The dataset includes:

* Order transactions
* Product categories
* Pricing and discount information

After data cleaning, approximately 40,000 valid orders were used for analysis.

---

## Key Insights

* Discounts are applied to the majority of sales
* No strong positive relationship between higher discounts and higher revenue
* Mid-range products dominate in volume, but higher-priced products generate more revenue
* Discount impact differs significantly across product categories

---

## Recommendations

* Avoid applying discounts uniformly across all products
* Focus on targeted promotions during key sales periods
* Adjust discount strategies based on product category
* Limit deep discounts on high-value products to protect margins
